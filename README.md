# Ars NumericHUD
Available on:
- Curseforge
- Modrinth

## What does it do?
Ars NumericHUD is a fully client-side mod that adds numbers to your mana bar to indicate how much mana you have, and also adds tooltips to the glyphs to show how much mana they use.
